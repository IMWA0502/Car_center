DRIVE

Please remember this is a second-hand vehicle and it will drive accordingly.
Parts do wear out, and you may need to renew or replace some over time.
Typical items that may require attention include:


  • Tyres
  • Flywheel / Clutch
  • Brake pads / Discs
  • Ball joints / Top mounts
  • Wheel bearings
  • Bushes
  • Chips or cracks in the windscreen
  • Electrical items
  • Warning lights on the dashboard

There are many electrical components in modern vehicles, and unfortunately, we do not have the time to test them all.
Some features that may not be fully functional include:


  • Parking sensors
  • Air conditioning
  • Electric windows, etc.
